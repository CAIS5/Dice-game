Dice Game

RULE :

A simple dice game, where you just roll a dice and get a score according to a dice face. A player who reaches to a target score before another player is winner.There are two modes :

  • 1 player : which you versus computer
  • 2 player : which you versus another player

During a game, you can choose a raise to double wining score,but there is a penalty if you lose. You will lose half of score you got so far.
